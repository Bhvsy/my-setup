Build Philosophy

This rig wasn't assembled for benchmarks -- it was engineered for execution. Every component was chosen to serve the three pillars of the Bhvsy OS ecosystem: trading infrastructure that doesn't lag under volatility, AI compute that runs locally without cloud dependency, and development throughput that ships tools fast. The RTX 4060 sits at the center -- handling GPU-accelerated charting, CUDA inference for Stable Diffusion, and DLSS-powered gaming sessions when the markets close.
